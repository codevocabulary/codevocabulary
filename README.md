### 🧠 The Philosophy
Syntax changes, but **logic is forever**. **CodeVocabulary** is dedicated to stripping away the complexity of programming to reveal the simple patterns underneath. 

Whether it's an `if` statement or a complex `algorithm`, we break it down into plain English.

---

### 🧱 The Building Blocks
I teach the fundamental "vocabulary" that every developer needs, regardless of the language they use:

* **Variables & Data:** How machines remember things.
* **Control Flow:** How programs make decisions.
* **Functions:** The art of reusable logic.
* **Algorithms:** The recipes for solving problems.
* **Data Structures:** How to organize information efficiently.
* **Debugging:** The science of finding out "what went wrong."

---

### 📂 What's Inside?
* 🗺️ **Roadmaps:** Step-by-step paths from "Hello World" to "Problem Solver."
* 📝 **Visual Guides:** Diagrams that explain logic better than words can.
* 💻 **Universal Examples:** Concepts explained so simply they apply everywhere.
